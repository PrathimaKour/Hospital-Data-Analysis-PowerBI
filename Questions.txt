Hospitals generate large amounts of operational and financial data daily. 
This project aims to analyze patient volume, doctor availability, medical expenses, hospital performance, and department efficiency using Power BI.

The dashboard helps identify operational trends, expense distribution, staffing workload, and healthcare demand across cities and departments.

1. Monitor patient and doctor distribution
2. Analyze medical expenses across hospitals and cities
3. Track monthly healthcare expense trends
4. Evaluate department-wise patient load
5. Identify operational inefficiencies
6. Support hospital management decision-making

| KPI                | DAX                       |
| ------------------ | ------------------------- |
| Total Patients     | SUM(Patients_count)       |
| Total Expenses     | SUM(Medical_Expenses)     |
| Avg Stay Days      | DATEDIFF avg              |
| Total Doctors      | SUM(Doctors_count)        |
| No. of Departments | DISTINCTCOUNT(Department) |
